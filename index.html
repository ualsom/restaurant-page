About Us

Welcome to Delight Bistro, a place where passion for fine dining and culinary expertise meets a relaxed, cozy atmosphere. With a commitment to the finest ingredients, every dish at Bistro Delights is a celebration of flavor and creativity.

Menu

  • Truffle Risotto - Creamy risotto cooked with black truffle and parmesan cheese. $18
  • Lamb Shank - Tender slow-cooked lamb shank served with mashed potatoes and red wine reduction. $25
  • Seafood Paella - Traditional Spanish paella with shrimp, mussels, and squid. $22
  • Pistachio Creme Brulee - Rich custard topped with caramelized sugar and pistachios. $10

Meet Our Chef

Chef Alexandre Dupont, originally from Provence, France, brings a world of culinary expertise to Bistro Delights. With over 25 years of experience working in Michelin-starred restaurants, Chef Dupont blends tradition with innovation to craft memorable dining experiences.
